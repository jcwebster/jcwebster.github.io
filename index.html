PROJECTS

Electric Skateboard Dynamometer

I designed and built an electric skateboard dyno operated by Arduino for R&D and analysis purposes, capable of measuring the speed and power of any rear-wheel drive electric skateboard on today’s market. It involved developing an automated C program for an Arduino Nano and an Excel template to analyze power curves of motors.

CFD Analysis of Airflow over Drone Fuselage

As a group we analyzed how the profile of a drone fuselage affects lift and drag coefficients during flight, applying the skills learned in our MTE 204 Numerical Methods course. The results were calculated using a NACA0012 airfoil profile drawn in ANSYS Fluent and presented in our video along with animations and real-life examples of where this research could apply.
