CFD Analysis of Airflow over Drone Fuselage

As a group we analyzed how the profile of a drone fuselage affects lift and drag coefficients during flight, applying the numerical methods learned in our MTE 204 Numerical Methods course. The results were calculated using a NACA0012 airfoil profile drawn in ANSYS Fluent and presented in our video along with animations and real-life examples of where this research could apply.
